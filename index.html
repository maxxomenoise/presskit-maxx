BIOGRAPHY

MAXX is an MC from Lampung, Indonesia, who kicked off his career in August 2023. Starting from local stages in his hometown, he quickly made a name for himself with high energy, crowd control, and a natural stage presence that’s hard to ignore.

In January 2024, he moved to Jakarta to chase bigger stages and bolder dreams—and it paid off. Now an official Talent MC under Holywings Group (HWG), MAXX continues to bring unforgettable vibes to every event.
